Potionomics is a PC video game about the business side of potion brewing.

Assume the role of a penniless witch named Sylvia who must transform her debt-ridden potion shop into a thriving business. Along the way, you will encounter mighty (and not-so-mighty) heroes, classic RPG creatures, and a colorful cast of fellow merchants. As every aspiring entrepreneur knows, customers will be fickle, competition will be fierce, and prices never stay stable. Potion brewing is as much a science as it is an art. Master your craft while learning new recipes along the way! Add in some magic and you have a recipe for a magnificent adventure unlike any you’ve ever played before.
